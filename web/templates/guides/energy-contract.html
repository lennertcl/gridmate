Energy Contract Guide

How to model your energy pricing for accurate cost calculations — Go to configuration

Field Explanations

How It Works

Your energy contract is modeled as a list of components. Each component represents one line item on your energy bill.

Component Types

Constant
A fixed fee charged per period regardless of usage. Examples: monthly network fee, yearly grid access fee.
  • Price: Amount in € per period
  • Period: Monthly or yearly
Fixed
A per-kWh charge at a fixed price. Used for fixed-rate consumption or injection rewards.
  • Price: €/kWh
  • Energy Sensor: Which consumption/injection counter to use (from your energy feed sensors or a predefined option)
  • Is Injection Reward: This component reduces your bill (e.g., solar feed-in tariff)
Variable
A per-kWh charge where the price comes from a dynamic HA sensor
  • Price Sensor: HA entity providing the current price (e.g., sensor.nordpool_kwh_be_eur)
  • Price Formula: multiplier × sensor_price[t] × usage[t] + constant — with varying energy prices over time.
  • Energy Sensor: Which consumption/injection counter to use (from your energy feed sensors or a predefined option)
  • Is Injection Reward: This component reduces your bill (e.g., solar feed-in tariff)
Capacity
A charge based on your peak power usage (capacity tariff). Typical in Belgian energy contracts. Billed per kW of your highest 15-minute average demand per period.
  • Capacity Price: €/kW per period
Percentage
A percentage surcharge applied on top of other components. Commonly used for VAT or levies.
  • Percentage: The rate (e.g., 6% or 21%)
  • Applies To: Select which other components this percentage is calculated on. E.g. typically injection rewards are not included in VAT calculation.

Multiplier

Every component has a multiplier (default: 1.0) that scales its contribution to the total cost. Use this to blend pricing mechanisms, e.g., set two components to 0.5 each for a 50/50 fixed/variable split.

Examples

Simple Fixed-Rate Contract

A basic contract suitable for most household users with predictable consumption.

Network Fee
Constant €12.00 / month
Energy Consumption
Fixed €0.225/kWh
Solar Injection Reward
Fixed €0.15/kWh injection reward (deducted from bill)
Total monthly bill:
  • + 12.00
  • + 0.225 × consumption
  • - 0.15 × injection

Combined Fixed + Variable Pricing

Advanced contract combining fixed rates, dynamic pricing, and capacity charges.

50% Fixed Consumption
Fixed €0.07/kWh, multiplier: 0.5
50% Variable Market Price
Variable 1.1 × BELPEX_RLP + €0.04/kWh, multiplier: 0.5
Grid Access Fee
Constant €18.00 / year
Energy Tax
Fixed €0.05/kWh
Capacity Charge
Capacity €53.00 / kW / year
Solar Injection Reward
Variable 0.2 × BELPEX_RLP injection reward
VAT (6%)
Percentage 6% on all components except Solar Injection Reward
Total monthly bill:
  • + 0.5 × 0.07 × consumption
  • + 0.5 × ∑(1.1 × BELPEX_RLP[t] + 0.04) × consumption[t]
  • + 18.00 / 12
  • + 0.05 × consumption
  • + 53.00 × peak_kW / 12
  • + 6% on all above
  • - ∑(0.2 × BELPEX_RLP[t] × injection[t])

Note: Multipliers allow flexible scaling of component importance. They don't need to sum to 1.0 — they simply define the relative contribution of each component to the total cost. A multiplier of 0.5 means 50% of the component's value is included in the final calculation, allowing you to blend different pricing mechanisms flexibly.
