Welcome to GridMate

What is GridMate?

GridMate is a Home Assistant addon that gives you insight into your home energy usage and helps you optimize costs. It connects to your existing Home Assistant sensors and provides dashboards, cost tracking, and optional automated optimization via EMHASS.

Setup Order

Follow these guides in order to configure GridMate:

  1. Energy Feed — Connect your smart meter sensors (required)
  2. Solar Panels — Add solar production and forecast sensors (if applicable)
  3. Devices — Register smart devices for monitoring and automation, including your home battery
  4. Energy Contract — Model your energy pricing for cost calculations
  5. Optimization — Set up EMHASS for automated energy optimization (optional)
