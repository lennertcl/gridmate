Field Explanations

Solar Panel Sensors

These sensors come from your solar inverter integration in Home Assistant.

Actual Production
Instantaneous power output of your solar panels in kW. This is the primary sensor used for live dashboards and auto-calculated usage.
Energy Production Today
Cumulative solar energy produced today in kWh. Resets at midnight.
Energy Production Lifetime
Total cumulative solar energy produced over the system's lifetime in kWh.

Solar Forecast Provider

Choose a forecast provider to predict solar production for optimization and dashboards.

None
No external forecast — EMHASS uses its internal weather-based method.
Forecast.Solar
Uses the Forecast.Solar HA integration. Provide the "estimated power +24h" sensor entity ID. Free, ~24h forecast horizon.
Solcast
Uses the Solcast HACS integration. Provide the forecast_today sensor entity ID. High accuracy, up to 48h horizon.
Naive (yesterday as forecast)
Uses yesterday's actual production data as the forecast. No external service needed — just your inverter's production sensor.
